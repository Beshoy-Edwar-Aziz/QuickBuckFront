Who Are We?

Specialized hiring platform for college students and fresh graduates in Egypt Brief Description of the Business Idea: QuickBuck is an innovative online platform dedicated to connecting college students and fresh graduates in Egypt with relevant entry-level job opportunities. Unlike traditional job portals, QuickBuck is tailored specifically for young job seekers at the start of their careers, ensuring they are not overshadowed by more experienced candidates. The platform provides a dedicated space where companies can efficiently find and recruit entry-level talent and interns, thereby streamlining the hiring process and improving employment outcomes for students and fresh graduates.
